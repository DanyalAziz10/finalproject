Who We Are

Islamia Medical Institute & College (IMIC) is committed to providing high-quality education in the field of medical sciences. We focus on preparing our students for a future in healthcare, with a strong emphasis on practical skills and knowledge.

What We Do

At IMIC, we offer a range of programs designed to equip students with the skills they need to succeed in the medical field. Our programs include Pre-Medical, Pre-Engineering, Computer Science, and more. We also provide paid courses in various subjects, including advanced computer skills, English language proficiency, and the sciences.
